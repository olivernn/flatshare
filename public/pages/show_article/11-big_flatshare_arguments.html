Big Flatshare Arguments

Whether you like it or not, they will happen, nine times out of ten you will end up in a massive argument with your flat mates over one of the following things -

  • Cleaning (or lack of it)
  • Noise too late at night
  • Food/drink etc going “missing”
  • Friends/partners staying over too often/constantly
  • Bathroom abuse

The reasons for big flatshare arguments are almost always trivial but they can bring out such anger in people that you would think that the issues were a matter of life and death.

You are allowed to get mad with people, from time to time your flatmates might even be allowed to loose their temper with you. When situations like this occur it is always best to try and have a serious talk with every one in the house, a dreaded house meeting, so that everyone can feel that they have their opinion heard.

Don’t make any rash decisions, by walking out on a flatshare and moving somewhere else you are not going to ruin their lives, they’ll probably find another flatmate and you will probably be the topic of drunken laughs for your ex-flatmates for quite a while, better to sort it out and perhaps do the dishes every now and then….
