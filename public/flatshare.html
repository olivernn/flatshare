Flatshare Search

Use the search form above to search for flat share adverts. You can search by the maximum amount of rent you wish to pay and also the location you wish to live in. It is also possible to find flats within a certain distance of a tube or train station.

Matching adverts are displayed as markers on the map, use this to determine where the flats are located within the area.

To get a better idea of what the location is like you can search for amenities in the local area, using the search box on the map. Search for local bars, supermarkets, cafes, gyms restaurants.

Local Search

We will display information local to the advert you are looking at here. Use this to get a really good idea of the area around the flatshare.
