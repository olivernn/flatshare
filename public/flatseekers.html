Flatseeker Search

Use the search controls to your left to find people looking for flat shares. You can search on the amount someone is willing to pay, or the area they are interested in living.

By seeking out people who are actively looking for a flat you stand a much greater chance of finding the right flatmate for your flat share.

Look for people who are looking for flats in your area and once you find someone who you think would be interested in your flat contact them and ask them to come round and view your flat.
